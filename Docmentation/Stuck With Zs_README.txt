GAME NAME: STUCK WITH Zs
TEAM NAME: DotCGen
CLASS    : CSD1450s22



Installation:
1. Unzip the folder and its contents to your preferred location.
2. Navigate to Installer > INSTALLER.
3. Run the Stuck With Zs_setup executable.
4. Accept the license agreement and click Next to proceed.
5. Choose your preferred installation location and click Next to proceed.
6. Click Next on the following screen to proceed.
7. You may choose if you would like a desktop shortcut created for you. Click Next to proceed.
8. Review the Tasks the installer will perform and click Install to proceed.
9. Once the installer has completed the installation, You may choose to automatically run the program.
10. Click on Finish to end the installation process.

How to play:
WASD for movement
R to reload
Z to switch between weapons
LMB or RMB to fire when Pistol is selected
LMB and RMB to fire when Bullet Storm is selected

Survive the zombie onslaught!

THERE ARE NO CHEAT CODES IN THE GAME.
WHEN FACED WITH A SELECTION,
AN EGG WILL COME TO LIGHT WHEN DotCGen IS HELD DOWN.
